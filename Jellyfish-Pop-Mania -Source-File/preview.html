Welcome to Jellyfish Pop Mania!

About Jellyfish Pop Mania

Embark on a mesmerizing underwater adventure like never before! Jellyfish Pop Mania is an addictive and visually stunning game that challenges your skills and strategy. Your mission is to save the ocean from the invasion of mischievous jellyfish. Can you handle the challenge?

Key Features

  • Engaging Gameplay:Pop jellyfish as they swim to protect the ocean's inhabitants.
  • Challenging Levels:Test your skills across a variety of levels with challenging gameplay. As you advance, brace yourself for faster and more agile jellyfishes that will put your reflexes to the test!
  • High Scores:Compete with players to claim the top spot on the leaderboards.

Game Overview

In Jellyfish Pop Mania, your objective is to eliminate all the jellyfish as they swim. Help Spongebob eliminate the jellyfishes before they eliminate him. Make precise and quick moves to save the ocean's tranquility.
